## Main Idea
Online student shop
Maybe for dorm students who are moving out and want to make extra cash UH Marketplace

## Overview
- Problem: Students need money, and also need to get rid of things, especially for students that dorm on campus.
- Solution: A way for students to make money, and less trash for the environment and dorms. Helps relieve stress because dorms have to move out during finals week.

## Names of the proposers
Darilyn Evangelista, Andrew Narciso

## Mockup page ideas
- Landing page
- User home page
- Admin home page (can control listings & support users)
- User profile page / user reviews page
- Shop Listing page
- Add Listing page
- Support Page (for help)
- Browse listings by price, recency, tag/category

## Use case ideas
- Add Listing page: Dorm students moving out during finals can sell unneeded furniture by adding their item as a listing.
- Shop Listing page: A student looking for cheaper and more accessible materials can easily find their desired item by searching and filtering what they want.
- Admin Home Page: Admins can remove any scammers/inappropriate items by looking through the database.

## Beyond the basics
- Seller rating system
- Internal messaging system for communication
- Email notification integration when someone is interested
